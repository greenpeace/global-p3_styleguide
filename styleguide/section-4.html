4.1 Generic button.

We support <input[submit]>, <input.button>, <button> and <a.button>

Compatible in IE6+, Firefox 2+, Safari 4+, Chrome 1+, Opera 11+

a.button
:hover

Subtle hover highlight and box shadow

a.button
:active

Inset box shadow and 1px offset

a.button
.primary

Primary buttons are orange

a.button
.disabled

Dims the button to indicate it cannot be used

a.button
.disabled:hover

Disabled buttons are inactive (no hover effect)

a.button
.disabled:active

Disabled buttons inactive (no active state)
