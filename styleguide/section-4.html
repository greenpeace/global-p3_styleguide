4 Buttons

4.1 Generic button.

We support <input[submit]>, <input.button>, <button> and <a.button>

Compatible in IE6+, Firefox 2+, Safari 4+, Chrome 1+, Opera 11+

a.button
:hover

Subtle hover highlight and box shadow

a.button
:focus

Subtle hover highlight and box shadow

a.button
:active

Inset box shadow and 1px offset

a.button
.primary

Primary buttons are orange

a.button
.primary:hover

Primary buttons are orange

a.button
.primary:focus

Primary buttons are orange

a.button
.primary:active

Primary buttons are orange

a.button
.disabled

Dims the button to indicate it cannot be used

a.button 
<input type="submit" class="{$modifiers}" value="input[submit]" />
<input class="button {$modifiers}" value="input.button" />
<button class="{$modifiers}">button</button>
<a class="button {$modifiers}">a.button</a>
